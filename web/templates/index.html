Paper Trail

"A book read by a thousand different people is a thusand different books." - Andre Tarkovsky.



We Challenge You to Read, Reflect, and Share!

Paper Trail is a letter sharing platform that transforms the lonesome reading experience into a social journey. We challenges the book lovers of the world to read a book that you always wanted to read, collect your reflections, and then share the book with someone in your network. A friend, a colleague, a crush, or a stranger. The choice is yours.



Explore Six Degrees of Connection Through a Book!

According to Six Degrees of Separation (no, not the title of the Irish rock band The Script's hit single), everyone is just six, or fewer, social connections away from one another. Granted, we want to stay as far as we want from each other right now, but jokes aside, imagine where your book could end up through your network? In the hands of a masked vigilante, your favourite singer, or maybe even, the gorgeous Prime Minister of New Zealand? Who knows! Paper Trail will help you to track your book along the way.



We build a webpage for each book that you share!

Paper Trail gets you started with a Book Profile for each book that you share, so you can always track your book's status. On this page, you can read what other book recievers' have written. Check out our sample page to see what a book page looks like.
