We Challenge You to Read, Reflect, and Share!

Paper Trail challenges you to read a book that you always wanted to read, reflect along when reading the book, then share the book with one of your connections.



Explore Six Degrees of Connection Through a Book!

Challenge your friend to read and share the book to one of their connections. Repeat the process until you get to the six degrees of connection. Paper Trail will help you to track your book along the way!



We build a webpage for each book that you share!

Paper Trail helps you to build a page for each book that you share, so you can always track your book's status. On this page, you can read through all book recievers' letter. Check out our sample page to see what a book page looks like.
